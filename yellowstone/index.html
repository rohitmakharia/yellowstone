05

Food & restaurants

Where 12 people eat across eight days. Strategy: pack lunches from the rental kitchens, sit-down dinners in town, group cooking at the rental on the heavy nights.

Daily · Lunches

Pack from the rental kitchen

Sit-down restaurants inside the parks have 60–90 minute waits and mediocre food. The rentals have full kitchens — use them.

  • Pack the night before. Sandwiches, fruit, snacks, water. One vehicle is the daily "cooler car."
  • Picnic at every scenic pullout. Jenny Lake, West Thumb, Old Faithful, Canyon Village all have designated picnic areas.
  • Backup cafeterias if needed. Old Faithful Lodge Cafeteria, Canyon Lodge Cafeteria — walk-in, no reservations, casual.
Evenings · Jackson

Town dinners in Jackson

Aug 25 is the planned group dinner night out. Late-August reservations for 12 are tight — book now.

  • Snake River GrillUpscale, classic Jackson institution. American/wood-fired. Reserve a private table.
  • GloriettaModern Italian, wood-fired. Strong wine list, good for a 12-top.
  • Persephone BakeryCasual upscale, French-leaning. Better for an earlier dinner or breakfast.
  • Hand Fire PizzaPizza + salads, easy for a group with kids. No-fuss.
  • Bin 22 / Cafe GenevieveWine bar tapas and a historic-log-cabin dinner spot. Solid alternates.
In-park · Special meals

Inside Yellowstone & the Tetons

A couple of in-park meals worth planning around — for the lobby experience, not just the food.

  • Old Faithful Inn Dining RoomAug 27 lunch — the 1904 log-cathedral lobby is the destination. Call when you book lodging; for 12 you'll need staggered reservations.
  • Jackson Lake LodgeAug 24 lunch — the picture window in the lobby alone is worth it. Mural Room or Pioneer Grill.
  • Corbet's Cabin (top of Tram)Aug 25 lunch — famous waffles at 10,450 ft. Quick service, no reservations.
  • Canyon Lodge dining roomAug 28 lunch — walk-in cafeteria style, easy with a group.
Stocking · Groceries

Where to grocery shop

Two stops total: one on Aug 22 night in Jackson, one on Aug 26 afternoon when you arrive in West Yellowstone.

  • Jackson · Albertsons24 hours. Most selection. South of Town Square.
  • Jackson · Jackson Whole GrocerUpscale, smaller crowds, better produce and prepared food. Closes 9 PM.
  • West Yellowstone · Market Place FoodsLocal supermarket, full grocery range.
  • West Yellowstone · Food RoundupSmaller backup option in town.
